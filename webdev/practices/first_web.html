Dirt Bike basics

Riding a dirt bike can be difficult but it will be worth it

Riding dirt bikes can be an expinsive sport in every way. First of all if you want to get into dirtbiking then you need to buy a machine, When looking for a bike to buy you will encounter many differnt brands which you can buy from, Some of the most famous and popular brands are Yamaha, Honda, Kawasaki, and KTM all of these bike brands are used by people all around the world and these bikes are the most reliable. The only issue with these bike brands is that they can be very expensive ranging from four thousand dollars all the way to fifteen thousand dollars meaning that just for the bike you need to spend at least six thousand dollars or more. Even after you buy a bike you still need to spend more money on the bike every couple of hours you need to do maintanence to the bike like doing oil changes and changing air filters, you will also need a trailer or at least a truck to move around your bike. all the newer bikes have an E start which is a button that you click to start the bike, if your bike does not have that then the bike will have a kickstart and you will have to kick start the bike to turn it on.


Some things you should know about riding

Every bike uses a clutch and gears to move once you turn on your bike and want to start going you hold the clutch lever in and put it in first gear, to put it in first gear you just push the shifter down then from there every single time that you want to go up a gear you just do the same process hold in the clutch and push the gear up one. Most of the bikes have five gears, but some of them have up to six gears. There are many differnt bikes that you can choose from its just a matter of which one you like and can handle. When you learn how to properly use your bike and have gotten more experience with it you can learn many differnt tricks or skills that can help you when you are riding. A skill that people should learn is that whenever you are riding you should keep two fingers on your clutch and brake lever, this just helps you ride a lot better and a lot of the motocross riders do this, Something else that you should learn to do is clutch control this will help you in a lot of situation when your riding. Other things that you should know how to do are things like fixing your bike and doing maintanence, so that you dont have to take it to a shop whenever it needs a oil change or anything because shops can charge a lot of money.
